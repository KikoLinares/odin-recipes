Aqua Glacier

The most refreshing way to feel like royalty. Using the finest of water from the top of mountains will make you feel as if you are drinking silk. Although it might seem complicated to make, it is well worth it in the end.

Ingedients

  • 3 cubes of Glacier at below freezing temperature
  • 250ml of fine spring Aqua or Water
  • Crystal cylinder or a glass

Traditional Procedure

  1. Drop your 3 cube Glaciers into teh cystal container.
  2. Delicately pour your spring Aqua over top the Glacial Cubes
  3. Let your beautiful creation rest for 1 minute and let the crystal absorbe the cold of the Aqua Glacial
  4. Enjoy

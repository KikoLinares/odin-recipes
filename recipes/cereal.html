Honey Drizzled Milk Enriched Oats

This Delicate dish will provide an experience unlike any before. Leaving the honey drizzled oats to soak in the milk will provide the perfect texture of crunch and silky soft milk. Many greats thorughout history have endulged in this treat, and now you finally have the chance to finally feel like royalty.

Ingedients

  • Honey Drizzled Oats, or Honey Nut Cheerios
  • Chilled Cow Milk, of the top 2 percentile
  • Fine China Bowl

Traditional Procedure

  1. Gracefully pour a cup of your Honey Oats in your Fine China Bowl
  2. Carefully pour your elite milk
  3. Let the beautiful oats and milk amalgamate for 20 seconds
  4. Enjoy

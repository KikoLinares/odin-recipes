Grilledcheese

The finest of cheese and bread will make you the talk of your gated community. The Jefforsons can do nothing but give you praise as they can only watch as you bite in perfection. Crunch, heat and the buttery after taste is to die for.

Ingedients

  • 3 slices of the finest Gruyere or Cheshire or American cheese
  • 2 slics of enriched sourdough prism bread
  • Fine Cows Butter

Traditional Procedure

  1. Heat a saucepan on medium heat
  2. Gently drop your Fine Cow Butter into the pan and let melt for 2 minutes
  3. Lay the slices of cheese on your sourdough bread and cover cheese with second slice of bread
  4. Place the sandiwch on the butter and cook until the crust is a perfect golden brown.
  5. Flip the sandwich and do the same to the other side
  6. Enjoy
